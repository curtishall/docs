- **MSG** — Send text to a specific unit: `MSG 423 Stage at rear door`.
- **BC** — Broadcast to all logged-in units (use carefully).
- **DNOTES** — Dispatcher notes attached to a CFS: `DNOTES #26-00045 Supervisor notified`.

Who sees each message depends on your mobile and CAD integrations; units typically see **MSG**/**BC** in their MDT workflow.
