Use this guide when something in Dispatch CAD isn't behaving as expected — a board that looks empty, an F-key that fires nothing, or a query button that never appears. Most issues resolve with a configuration check in Web Admin or a data reload; work through the relevant accordion below before escalating to Cadmus support.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Cannot sign in">
    If you cannot sign in to Dispatch CAD, check the three most common causes before contacting your administrator.

    1. **Account inactive** — ask your agency admin to verify your account status in **Web Admin → Users** and reactivate it if needed.
    2. **Wrong environment** — confirm whether you are trying to sign in to the Live or Sandbox organization. The sign-in screen displays the organization name; look for a "Sandbox" or "Training" label.
    3. **Invite pending** — if your account was recently created, check your email for an invitation link. If the invite expired, ask your admin to resend it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Call boards are empty">
    Your boards can appear empty for three common reasons: an incorrect agency filter, the wrong organization selected, or stale cached data.

    1. Open the **Select Agencies** picker in the header and confirm that the correct agencies are included. If you added or were assigned to an agency recently, it may not be checked.
    2. Verify the organization badge in the header. If you belong to multiple organizations (for example, a Live org and a Sandbox training org), confirm you are signed in to the correct one.
    3. Select **Reload Units & Dashboard Data** from the header settings menu to force a fresh pull from the server.

    <Tip>
      If calls reappear after a reload but disappear again within minutes, contact your admin — the issue may be a WebSocket connectivity problem rather than a filter setting.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Incident types missing from New CFS">
    When you open **New CFS** (F1) and expected incident types are absent, the type may be inactive in Web Admin or the local data cache is stale.

    1. Ask your admin to navigate to **Web Admin → Incident types** and confirm the type is set to **Active**.
    2. After the admin activates or adds the type, select **Reload Data** from the header settings menu in Dispatch CAD. New incident types populate only after a reload.
    3. Confirm you are in the correct organization — incident types are org-scoped, so switching to a different org will show a different list.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quick call F-key does nothing">
    If pressing an F-key shortcut (F8–F12) produces no action, the associated template is likely missing or its linked incident type is inactive.

    1. Ask your admin to open **Web Admin → Quick Call Shortcuts** and verify a template exists for that key.
    2. Confirm the incident type linked to that template is **Active** in Web Admin.
    3. Select **Reload Data** in Dispatch CAD after any template changes. If the template was just created, a full restart of Dispatch CAD may be required before the shortcut fires.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="NCIC/Query button not appearing">
    The **Query** button (F3) is controlled by a combination of agency-level and org-level settings. If it is not visible, one or both settings are likely off.

    1. Your admin must enable **State/NCIC Access** in **Web Admin → Agencies → your agency → Settings**.
    2. **State/NCIC Access** requires a CJIS site to be configured at the organization level by a CadmusAdmin. If the toggle is on but the button still does not appear, the org-level CJIS site may be missing — contact your CadmusAdmin.
    3. If your agency has NCIC enabled but your personal account does not, ask your admin about a user-level override.

    <Warning>
      Only CJIS-certified personnel should be granted NCIC access. Contact your agency's CJIS security officer before requesting this change.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Units not showing in Login User (F2)">
    If the **Login User** dialog (F2) shows an empty unit list, either the wrong agency is selected or units have not been created in Web Admin.

    1. Confirm the agency selected in the Dispatch CAD header matches the agency whose units you expect to see. Units are agency-scoped.
    2. Ask your admin to navigate to **Web Admin → Agencies → your agency → Units** and verify that units have been created and are active.
    3. Select **Reload Data** after the admin adds or activates units.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Sandbox vs. Live confusion">
    Cadmus deployments commonly pair a **Live** organization for production use with a **Sandbox** organization for training. Dispatching in the wrong environment is a significant risk.

    1. Check the header banner — Dispatch CAD displays a prominent **SANDBOX MODE** or **LIVE MODE** indicator.
    2. If you are unsure which environment you signed in to, sign out and confirm the organization name on the sign-in screen. Sandbox orgs are typically labeled with a suffix such as "– Sandbox" or "– Training."
    3. Never record real citizen calls or submit NCIC queries against real plates in a Sandbox org.

    <Note>
      If your organization's Live and Sandbox orgs have similar names, ask your OrgAdmin to rename them more distinctly to prevent confusion.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Map or RapidSOS not working">
    If the map view (F4) fails to load or the RapidSOS caller-location feature is unavailable, check the following:

    1. Confirm that GIS and RapidSOS integrations are configured at the organization level in **Web Admin → Integrations**.
    2. The RapidSOS header shortcut may be unavailable in some live deployments — use the map-side RapidSOS tracking panel instead.
    3. If a call's map pin is not resolving to the correct location, try **Refresh Address Resolution** on the call detail before pinning the location on the map.

    <Note>
      Contact your OrgAdmin if GIS or RapidSOS integrations do not appear in Web Admin — these may need to be enabled at the organization level.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="WebSocket updates appear stuck">
    If the call board or unit statuses stop refreshing in real time, the WebSocket connection may have dropped.

    1. Select **Reload Data** from the header settings gear menu. This reconnects the WebSocket and re-fetches all current data.
    2. If the problem persists, open the gear menu and run **Test Connection** to check server reachability.
    3. As a last resort, use **Force Reload** (also in the gear menu) to perform a full page reload without losing your current session.

    <Warning>
      **Clear All Caches** (available in Settings & Tools) may sign you out or reset local preferences. Use it only when directed by Cadmus support.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Escalate to Cadmus support

If none of the steps above resolve your issue, contact Cadmus support. To help the team investigate quickly, collect the following information before you reach out:

- **Organization name** and whether it is Live or Sandbox
- **Agency name**
- **Affected user email address**
- **Time the issue occurred** (include time zone)
- **Steps to reproduce** the problem
- **Screenshots** showing the unexpected behavior

<Warning>
  Never include NCIC response data, driver's license numbers, plate results, or any other CJIS-protected information in support tickets, screenshots, or screen recordings. Doing so may violate your agency's CJIS Security Policy.
</Warning>
