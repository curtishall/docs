Voice Alerts lets the MDT app speak important information aloud.

## What it can announce

- **Incoming call alerts** — When a new call is assigned to you
- **CJIS/NCIC result summaries** — Person/driver and vehicle search results

## Enabling voice alerts

1. Open **Settings** (gear icon or menu).
2. Find the **Voice Alerts** section.
3. Turn on the toggles you want:
   - **Incoming call alerts**
   - **CJIS result summaries**

## Incoming call alerts

When enabled:

- The app announces each assigned call as it appears.
- Default phrase: **"You have a call."**
- You can change the phrase in Settings (e.g. "New call assigned").

## CJIS/NCIC result summaries

When enabled:

- **Person/driver searches**: Speaks driver name, license status, and warrant information.
- **Vehicle searches**: Speaks make/model and registration status.
- **Replay**: Use the **LISTEN** button in the NCIC screen to hear the selected result again.

## Tips

- Use your **device volume** for voice alert loudness.
- Turn off voice alerts in Settings when you need silence.
- Keep custom call phrases short so they're easy to hear over background noise.

## Requirements

- NCIC voice summaries require agency **State/NCIC Access** and your user to be authorized (configured in Web Admin).
