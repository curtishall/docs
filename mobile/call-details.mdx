## Status buttons (on assigned call)

When your unit is on the call:

| Button | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **DISPATCHED** | Acknowledge assignment |
| **EN ROUTE** | En route to scene |
| **ON SCENE** | Arrived on scene |
| **CLEAR CALL** | Clear your unit from call |

Requires network — offline shows **No network connection. Try again when online.**

## History & Logs

- Scroll to **History & Logs** on call detail (phone) or the detail panel (tablet/desktop).
- **Add a note** inline or use the app bar note icon / **Dialog** link.
- **Refresh** icon reloads notes and system log lines.
- Notes need network — offline attempts show a clear error and do not claim success.
- Detail screen **updates automatically** when dispatch adds notes or changes status (WebSocket refresh).
- Notes that contain pasted CJIS/MULES/NCIC/NLETS/DOR response headers are marked **RESTRICTED** for non–Law Enforcement viewers.

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: History & Logs section with note field</Note>

## Android Auto

When connected to Android Auto, **My Calls** shows your assignments with critical/ProQA labels. Call detail shows address, status, **Navigate**, status buttons, and the **latest note** (one short line — not the full timeline).

## Add persons and vehicles

- **Add Person to Call** / **ADD PERSON**
- **Add Vehicle to Call** / **ADD VEHICLE**
- Required: **First and last name are required**, **License plate is required**
- When dispatch adds or updates a person or vehicle on the same open call, mobile refreshes the **Persons involved** and **Vehicles involved** sections automatically (and vice versa on the dispatch CAD).
