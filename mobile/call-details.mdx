## Status buttons (on assigned call)

When your unit is on the call:

| Button | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **DISPATCHED** | Acknowledge assignment |
| **EN ROUTE** | En route to scene |
| **ON SCENE** | Arrived on scene |
| **CLEAR CALL** | Clear your unit from call |

Requires network — offline shows **No network connection. Try again when online.**

## Review CFS entries from Find a Call

When call details are opened from **Find a Call**, the header shows your position in the result set, such as **3 of 25** or **26 of 142**.

- **Previous** opens the prior CFS in the same search results.
- **Next** opens the next CFS in the same search results.
- **Enter** and the **right arrow** also move to the next CFS.
- The **left arrow** moves to the previous CFS.

Shortcuts only apply while reviewing details. They do not run while your cursor is in a text field, notes dialog, or other editable control.
