## Standalone vs call-linked citations

| Agency setup | Mobile behavior |
|---|---|
| **CAD Module** on (default) | **Linked call** dropdown appears on new citations. **ISSUE CITATION** requires an assigned call for live sync. |
| **CAD Module** off, **E-Ticket Module** on | Calls, Map, and Dashboard nav are hidden. New citations do **not** ask for a call. Issue and sync work without an `IncidentID`. |

Configure modules under [Agency settings](/admin/agency-settings) in Web Admin.

## New citation wizard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose citation or warning">
    Tap **NEW CITATION** and toggle **CITATION** vs **WARNING**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete wizard steps">
    Fill **Subject / Vehicle**, **Violation / Loc** or **Violation** (template-driven forms may use this label), **Signatures**, and **Review & Issue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Violation charge codes">
    On **Violation / Loc** or **Violation**, tap **Search charge codes** or start typing in the violation description field. Type an offense description (for example, *financial responsibility*). Results are ranked by description, not RSMO number. Codes your agency uses often appear with an **Often used** badge. **Unlawful Act** is filled from the selection but can still be edited. If the catalog is not cached yet, connect once to sync, or enter the charge manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Issue or save draft">
    Tap **ISSUE CITATION** or **SAVE DRAFT**. Use **Violator Refused to Sign** when applicable. For full CAD agencies, select a **Linked call** before issuing for live sync. Standalone e-ticket agencies skip the call selector.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Status labels

- **PENDING SYNC** — waiting for network upload
- **SUBMITTED** — sent to records
- **PRINTED** — hard copy produced

## Print

- **PRINT** from preview

## Offline and sync

- Drafts stored locally on the device.
- Active charge codes for your agency **State** (for example MO/RSMO) are cached after the first online visit to **E-CITATIONS**. Charge code search works offline after that sync.
- **Auto-Sync Ticket Data** in Settings uploads when online.
- See [Connectivity and offline](/mobile/connectivity-offline).

## Requirements

- Agency **E-Ticket Module** enabled in Web Admin (on by default for existing agencies).
- Agency **Traffic Stops Allowed** in Web Admin for citation workflows.
- For call-linked citations: **CAD Module** on and an assigned call selected before issue.
- Bluetooth printer paired if printing in field.

## Warnings

<Warning>Issued citations are legal documents — verify identity and statute before **ISSUE CITATION**.</Warning>

<Warning>**PENDING SYNC** means records may not have citation yet — confirm sync before leaving stop.</Warning>
