## New citation wizard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose citation or warning">
    Tap **NEW CITATION** and toggle **CITATION** vs **WARNING**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete wizard steps">
    Fill **Subject / Vehicle**, **Violation / Loc**, **Signatures**, and **Review & Issue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Driver information">
    On **Subject / Vehicle** or **Driver**, tap **Scan ID** to scan the license barcode with the device camera or a hardware wedge scanner. Review the parsed fields, or tap **Use demo data** for training. Mapped fields include name, DOB, license number, and address. Sex, height, and similar fields not on the citation form are ignored.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Issue or save draft">
    Tap **ISSUE CITATION** or **SAVE DRAFT**. Use **Violator Refused to Sign** when applicable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Status labels

- **PENDING SYNC** — waiting for network upload
- **SUBMITTED** — sent to records
- **PRINTED** — hard copy produced

## Print

- **PRINT** from preview
- **BACK TO E-CITATIONS**
- PDF sections: **DEFENDANT / DRIVER**, **VEHICLE**, **OFFENSE**

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: Citation print preview</Note>

## Offline and sync

- Drafts stored locally on device.
- **Auto-Sync Ticket Data** in Settings uploads when online.
- See [Connectivity and offline](/mobile/connectivity-offline).

## Requirements

- Agency **Traffic Stops Allowed** in Web Admin.
- Bluetooth printer paired if printing in field.

## Warnings

<Warning>Issued citations are legal documents — verify identity and statute before **ISSUE CITATION**.</Warning>

<Warning>**PENDING SYNC** means records may not have citation yet — confirm sync before leaving stop.</Warning>
