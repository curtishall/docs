Documentation for the Cadmus mobile MDT app used by patrol deputies, fire, and EMS in the field. Mobile CAD shows the same calls dispatchers create in **Dispatch CAD** and reflects agency settings from **Web Admin**.

## Who this is for

- Patrol deputies and field responders
- Touchscreen MDT users
- Supervisors training new officers

## New officer: start here

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set your unit">
    Confirm your **call sign / unit** in [Profile and unit](/mobile/profile-and-unit) before shift.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your calls">
    Open [Calls list](/mobile/calls-list) — use **MY CALLS** for assignments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work a call">
    Tap a row for [Call details](/mobile/call-details); update [Unit status](/mobile/unit-status) as you respond.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close or hand off">
    Follow agency policy for [Close call](/mobile/close-call) or RTF (report to follow).
  </Step>
</Steps>
