## Quick log actions

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **NCIC QUERY** | Open NCIC query workspace (person, vehicle, firearm, article, boat) |
| **LOG PAPER SERVICE** | Log paper service event |
| **LOG BUSINESS CHECK** | Log business check |

**NCIC QUERY** stays visible even when your agency or user is not authorized for live NCIC. In that case the chip appears dimmed; tapping it shows:

`NCIC access is not enabled for either your agency or your user, contact your administrator`

Training mode still enables NCIC for practice queries without live CJIS access.

## Information panels

Typical panels (availability varies):

- **SHIFT ACTIVITY**
- **LOCATION**
- **ON-DUTY UNITS**
- **WEATHER**
- **SYSTEM HEALTH**

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: Dashboard panels grid</Note>

## Tips

- Set **AVAILABLE** here when starting shift.
- Use panels for situational awareness — not a substitute for dispatch radio.
- **SYSTEM HEALTH** helps field IT triage without calling support blind.

## Warnings

<Warning>Dashboard metrics may lag if **WS OFFLINE** — check connectivity.</Warning>
