Navigate to call locations on the MDT map.

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: Map screen with call pin and BASE MAP selector</Note>

## Open map

- Sidebar **Map**
- Or **OPEN MAP** from call detail

## Base map styles

- **BASE MAP** selector
- **NightOps (Dark)**
- **DayPatrol (Light)**

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: Map style toggle NightOps vs DayPatrol</Note>

## Navigation

- Map centers on call or GPS location when available.
- Error: **Unable to locate address on map** — verify address with dispatch.

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: Unable to locate address message</Note>

## Offline tiles

Map may use cached tiles on slow or offline connections — see [Connectivity and offline](/mobile/connectivity-offline).

## Tips

- Switch **NightOps** for night shifts to reduce glare.
- Confirm pin matches cross streets before proceeding.
- Use map for orientation — follow agency policy for en-route navigation apps.

## Warnings

<Warning>Driving while interacting with map may violate policy — park safe or use passenger/MDT mount.</Warning>
