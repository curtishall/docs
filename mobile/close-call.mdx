Close a call from the field MDT when authorized.

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: Close call dialog on mobile</Note>

## Open dialog

From call detail when your role/agency allows close:

- **Close call** action opens dialog.

## Fields

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Public narrative** | Incident summary for record |
| **Private notes** | Restricted notes when supported |
| **Report to follow (RTF)** | Flag for records follow-up |

## Submit

- **Close call** button
- Success: **Call closed** snackbar

<Note>Screenshot placeholder: RTF checkbox and Close call button</Note>

## Tips

- Match agency SOP for minimum narrative length.
- Use RTF when report number will be assigned later.
- Confirm with dispatch before closing multi-unit calls if you are not primary.

## Warnings

<Warning>Closing may lock notes and evidence uploads — confirm scene is complete.</Warning>

<Warning>Requires network — cannot close offline.</Warning>
