Find a call

Search closed and historical calls beyond the Recent board.

> Screenshot placeholder: Find a Call search dialog

## #Open Find a Call

- Header history icon — tooltip **Find a Call**
 
- Or from Recent tab: **Find a Call (full history)**
 

## #Search

- **Search calls** field — CFS number, address, keywords as supported.
 
- Pagination: **N / total** results.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Search results list with pagination

## #Open result

- Click result to open call detail (read-only or limited edit depending on status).
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Historical call detail opened from search

## #Tips

- Search by exact CFS number when known.
 
- Use address fragment for repeat-location research.
 
- Export or print from call detail if agency policy requires hard copy.
 

## #Warnings

> **Warning:** Historical calls may contain PII — handle per agency records policy.
