## #Send SMS from a call

Quick steps — for templates, merge fields, groups, and troubleshooting see [**SMS templates and messaging**](https://./sms-templates-and-messaging.md) (deep dive).

1. Open call detail.
 
2. **Send SMS** (toolbar).
 
3. Choose **Template**, edit **Message**, select **Groups** and/or **Individuals**.
 
4. **Preview** optional → **Send SMS**.
 
5. Review **SMS Send Results** and **SMS History** on the call.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Send SMS bottom sheet with template and recipients

## #SMS history

On call detail: **SMS History**

- Empty: **No SMS messages sent for this call**
 
- Shows delivery status for past messages.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: SMS History section on call

## #Command line messaging

See [Messaging commands](https://./command-line/messaging-commands.md):

- **MSG** — message one unit
 
- **BC** — broadcast (use carefully)
 
- **DNOTES** — dispatcher notes on CFS
 

## #Tips

- Use templates for consistent wording to field units.
 
- Confirm recipient group before broadcast-style messages.
 
- SMS requires an configured SMS service — admin toggles alone do not enable texting.
 

## #Warnings

> **Warning:** **BC** reaches all logged-in units — avoid sensitive info in broadcast text.

> **Warning:** SMS may not reach units without mobile data or registered MDT — confirm delivery in history.
