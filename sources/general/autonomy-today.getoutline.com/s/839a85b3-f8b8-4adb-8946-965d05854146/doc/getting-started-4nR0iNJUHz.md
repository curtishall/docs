Getting started

First-run orientation for dispatchers using the Cadmus Dispatch CAD client.

## #Sign in

1. Launch the Cadmus Dispatch CAD application.
 
2. Sign in with your agency credentials (Clerk authentication).
 
3. After sign-in, the **dashboard** loads with call boards and the units sidebar.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Sign-in screen (requires test credentials)

## #Organization and agency context

- Your **organization** determines which calls, units, and settings you see.
 
- Use **Select Agencies** in the header to filter boards when you work multiple agencies.
 
- Labels: **All Agencies**, **Clear**, **Done**.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Agency filter dropdown in header

## #Sandbox vs live

If your deployment includes training organizations:

- Header may show **SANDBOX MODE** or **LIVE MODE**.
 
- Always confirm which environment you are in before creating or closing real calls.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Sandbox/Live badge in header

## #Reload after admin changes

If incident types, quick calls, or units look stale:

1. Open header menu **Settings & tools** (gear).
 
2. Choose **Reload Units & Dashboard Data** (or **Force Reload**).
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Settings and tools menu with reload option

## #Tips

- Pin the window full-screen on dispatch workstations.
 
- Learn **F1–F5** before relying on mouse navigation.
 
- Use **HELP** in the command line for agency-specific aliases.
 

## #Warnings

> **Warning:** Do not dispatch in Sandbox if your agency treats that org as training-only — confirm SOP.

> **Warning:** Sign out (**Sign Out** in user menu) on shared workstations at shift change.
