BOLO, greaseboard, and wrecker

Operational boards and agency-wide resources.

## #Create BOLO

Header button **BOLO** opens broadcast dialog:

- **BROADCAST NEW BOLO**
 
- **SUBJECT INFORMATION**
 
- **VEHICLE INFORMATION**
 
- **SELECT IMAGE** (optional)
 
- **BROADCAST BOLO** to publish
 

> Screenshot placeholder: BROADCAST NEW BOLO dialog

## #BOLOs dashboard tab

**BOLOs** tab on dashboard:

- List of active BOLOs
 
- **Refresh**
 
- Detail: **BOLO DETAILS**, **DISMISS**, **EDIT**, **CANCEL BOLO**
 

> Screenshot placeholder: BOLOs tab with active list

## #Operational greaseboard

**Resources** tab → **Operational Board**

Categories may include:

- **Daily Operations**
 
- **Burn Permits**
 
- **Repos**
 
- **Hazards**
 
- **Wrecker Notes**
 
- **Shift Pass-On**
 

**Pinned Alerts** at top.

## #Tips

- Pin critical greaseboard items for shift pass-on.
 
- Refresh BOLO tab after creating new broadcast.
 
- Document wrecker rotation changes in shift log when SOP requires.
 

## #Warnings

> **Warning:** BOLO images may contain sensitive content — follow dissemination policy.
