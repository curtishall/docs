Call detail overview

The call detail page is the full record for one CFS.

> Screenshot placeholder: Call detail page — full layout with all major blocks visible

## #Open a call

- Click a call card on any dashboard tab.
 
- After **Create Call**, **Dispatch CAD** may navigate automatically.
 
- **Find a Call** for historical records.

## #Major blocks

### #Call summary

- Status, priority, type, recent updates.
 
- **Edit Call Details**, **RECENT UPDATES**.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Call Summary block

### #Call type and description

- **CALL DESCRIPTION**, **Edit Incident Type**.
 

### #Call intelligence

Tabs: **Activity**, **Location**, **Callers** — prior activity at address.

> Screenshot placeholder: Call intelligence tabs

### #Location details

- **Validate and Geocode Address**
 
- **Refresh Address Resolution**
 
- **Copy GPS coordinates**
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Location details block with map snippet

### #Units on call

- **Units (n)** with status chips: **AVAIL**, **DISP**, **ENRT**, **SCENE**, **CLR**
 
- **AVAILABLE UNITS** row for quick assign
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Units on call with status chips

### #Reporting party and callers

- **REPORTING PARTY** with **Query** for CJIS person lookup.
 
- **ADDITIONAL CALLERS**, **Add caller**.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Reporting party block

### #Persons and vehicles

- **PERSONS INVOLVED** — Suspect, Victim, Witness, Driver, Passenger, Other.
 
- **Add Vehicle**, **Run CJIS vehicle query**.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Persons involved list

### #Assigned agencies

- Multi-agency CFS numbers, **PRIMARY** agency.
 
- **Generate report #**, **Set Primary Agency**, **Remove Agency**.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Assigned agencies block

### #Agency status timers

- Per-agency timer cards on multi-agency calls.
 
- **Status Check Complete**, **Pause checks**, interval and snooze options.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Agency status timer card on call

### #History, logs, and images

- **Call Notes** vs **Audit Logs**.
 
- **Add Log**, **Add Call Note**.
 
- **Add Image**, **Take Photo** (paste/upload when supported).
 

> Screenshot placeholder: History and logs with Add Image buttons

### #Quick actions

- **Add Log Entry**, **Assign Unit**, **Assign Report Number**, **Clear Call**.
 

> Screenshot placeholder: Quick Actions column

## #Closed calls

When status is **Closed**:

- Editing and new logs/images may be restricted.
 
- Use **Reopen Call** only per agency policy.
